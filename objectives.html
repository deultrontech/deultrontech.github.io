Our Objectives

Objective 1

(a) To Glorify and Engulf in his Infinite Love and Buoyant Grace of the Lord Thiruchendur Murugan, with its roaring Sea, Skirting the altar of Lord Shanmuga abode ( thirucheeralaivai ), upon his Mercy, to been adored globally, irrespective of any Caste, Creed, Race and Religion.

Objective 2

(b) To Coordinate and Conduct globally in the Research, Renovation and Maintenance of Village Deities in their Shrines, which are quite often Simple and Non Vedic form of Worship in Nature, consisting of Folk Tales, Folk Songs and Folk Arts etc..

Objective 3

(c) To Organise and Provide “ Grand Feast ” ( Free Supply of hygienically and qualitatively food and drinking water ) during festival days in the Villages and Places of Worships. Especially, during auspicious Skandha Sasti Festival days for Millions of Enlightenment Spiritual Seekers, Hapless victims of both Old aged men and women left out, Destitute Widows with their little Childrens, Physically and Mentally Challenged left out, who will be thronging on this seashores of the divinely place, just for their bare Survivability of existence on these festival days.

Objective 4

(d) To Coordinate, Create , Conduct , Research and Awareness for Overall Well - Being of Humanity, Especially, towards the Upliftment of Marginalised Downtrodden Section of the Society in their Various Focused Fields as listed below, (i) Education : - To Motivate the Remote Regional Village Primary / Secondary School Students through distribution of Medals, Prizes, Certificates to School Toppers and School aided Kits. - To Create the Basic Awareness of Menstrual Hygiene and to identify Iron Deficiency Anemia conditions, among the Girl Students of Village Regional Schools, along with its providing of necessary hygiene products and its enriching iron and multivitamin Supplements. - To Create Essential Infrastructures like bore wells, electric motor and boards, water tanks, Reverse Osmosis (R.O) Drinking Water Treatment Plant etc.. inorder to ensure continuous and adequate supply of water, round the clock, which is the Most Basic Primarily needed tool towards ensuring the Safe and Hygienic Basic Sanitation facilities in any Remote Regional Village Schools.

Objective 5

(ii) Health Care : - To identify the Iron Deficiency Anemia Conditions, among the Womens and Childrens in the remote villages, through Health Camp, Check / Follow up procedures, along with its necessary supply of enriching Iron and Multivitamin Supplements.

Objective 6

(iii) Recreational Sports : - To Conduct Traditional Village Cultural Games, like drawing of Rangoli, Kolam patterns, Kabbadi, Rope Pulling etc.. among the Villagers of Illiterate Womens and Childrens, in the way of recognizing, honouring through Prizes and Certificates and to make them to actively involve and participate in the Global Society, where they are most often Segregated or Neglected, due to their Socio - Economic Conditions, thereby restoring the “ Dignity of Womanhood ” in all its spheres.

Objective 7

(iv) Arts and Literatures : - To Conduct Folk Tales & Poetry, Folk Stage Songs, Folk Stage Drama and Folk Arts etc.. in the Villages, inorder to foster the Creativity, Solidarity and Harmony among the Neighbourhood.

Objective 8

(v.) Environment : - To Conduct and Assist in the Research and Coordinate in all the levels of Agricultural and Livestock Activities, including providing Micro Credit to Small and Marginal Farmers and Agriculture Labourers.

The glory of Main Deity, in its temple of Lord Muruga, at the Tiruchendur is that, after the Soorasamharam event, the Lord Muruga was utterly absorbed in the Adoration of Lord Shiva by offering flowers with his hands.
