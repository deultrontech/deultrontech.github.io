The glory of Main Deity, in its temple of Lord Muruga, at the Tiruchendur is that, after the Soorasamharam event, the Lord Muruga was utterly absorbed in the Adoration of Lord Shiva by offering flowers with his hands.

TITLE

Meanwhile, an Celestial being immerged and astonished at Lord Muruga beauty of worship, cried out, in out of control as " Muruga " in an rustled Voice. When the Lord Muruga heard it, he immediately turned around, with his offering flowers in his hands, misperceiving that, they might have cried out his name, towards any needed help..

TITLE
