Objectives with its By-laws

Its Predominant Bye -laws are

The Trustees themselves, nor shall authorize anyone on its behalf, to raise or seek funds, grants, gifts, donations, loans etc.. in the way of advertising or propagating or publicizing it, in an Commercial Manner, through any forms of Printed or Web Media, including in its own website contents at any point of time.

By-Law 2

The Trustees themselves, nor shall authorize anyone on its behalf, to proclaim publicly its accomplished or achieved tasks, in the way of advertising or propagating or publicizing it, in an Commercial Manner, through any forms of Printed or Web Media, including in its own website contents at all times. However , its an Non Statutory Obligation, to intimate/ address its progressive developments and accomplished tasks to its donors and like minded peoples , in an Informal / Private manner, subjected to the resolutions accepted by its trustees.

By-Law 3

The Trustees themselves should not participate Personally nor accept Publicly, any kinds of Awards, Medals, Recognitions and Honours from the Public or Private Individuals, Government or Private Organisations, from both India and Abroad at any point of time. However in times of exceptional extreme exigency / crisis situations for its survival needs of its objectives, it may be accepted or considered with absolute passed resolution by its trustees.

The glory of Main Deity, in its temple of Lord Muruga, at the Tiruchendur is that, after the Soorasamharam event, the Lord Muruga was utterly absorbed in the Adoration of Lord Shiva by offering flowers with his hands.
